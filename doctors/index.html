Dr Andrew Mitchell - General Surgeon

new patients welcome

Please note that Dr Mitchell is a specialist and requires a valid referral from a GP for a surgical consultation to be eligible for a Medicare rebate. If you have an existing referral to another general surgeon and would like to be seen by Dr Mitchell, your existing referral will be accepted. You do not require your referral to be re-written.

Dr Mitchell specialises in a range of surgical services including:
Laparoscopic gall bladder removal
Hernia Repair
Skin Lesion Management
Comprehensive Anal Condition Care (Haemorrhoids, Fistula, Fissure)
Pilonidal Sinus Management
Lipoma Removal

Expertise in incidental liver and pancreas lesions
